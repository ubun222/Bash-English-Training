She enjoys her own company when she is travelling. 她喜爱独自旅行。
The children are very good company at this age. 和这个年龄的孩子在一起很开心。
He’s nervous in the company of his colleagues. 他和同事们一起时很紧张。
I’ll stay and keep you company. 我会留下来陪你。
I took my mother with me for company. 我把母亲带在身边做伴。
have the pleasure of sb’s company（正式用法）有幸邀请到某人光临
It is bad manners to whisper in company. 在众人面前窃窃私语是不礼貌的行为。
That’s not something to say in mixed company. 在男女都在场的情况下不该说那些话。
John’s mother was worried about the company he kept. 约翰的母亲很担心他交的那些朋友。
We’re expecting company this afternoon. 我们今天下午有客人。
