If you come or go to someone's aid, you try to help them when they are in danger or difficulty. 向某人提供帮助
An aid is an object, device, or technique that makes something easier to do. 辅助工具
If something aids a process, it makes it easier or more likely to happen. 促使; 有助于 (事情发生)
Aid is also a noun. 帮助
To aid someone means to help or assist them. 帮助
-aided 援助的
To aid a country, organization, or person means to provide them with money, equipment, or services that they need. 援助
If you perform a task with the aid of something, you need or use that thing to perform that task. 帮助
Aid is money, equipment, or services that are provided for people, countries, or organizations who need them but cannot provide them for themselves. 援助
