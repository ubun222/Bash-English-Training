You use instant to describe something that happens immediately. 立即的
To do something the instant something else happens means to do it immediately. 一…就…
If you say that something happens at a particular instant, you mean that it happens at exactly the time you have been referring to, and you are usually suggesting that it happens quickly or immediately. 时刻
