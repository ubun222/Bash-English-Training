Dictates are principles or rules which you consider to be extremely important. 原则; 规定
You say that logic or common sense dictates that a particular thing is the case when you believe strongly that it is the case and that logic or common sense will cause other people to agree. (逻辑或常识) 使人相信
If one thing dictates another, the first thing causes or influences the second thing. 导致; 影响
If someone dictates to someone else, they tell them what they should do or can do. 指示; 命令
If you dictate something, you say or read it aloud for someone else to write down. 口授; 使听写
