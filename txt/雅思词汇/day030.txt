If something that was lost or stolen is restored to its owner, it is returned to them. 归还
restoration 修复
When someone restores something such as an old building, painting, or piece of furniture, they repair and clean it, so that it looks like it did when it was new. 修复
restoration 复原
To restore someone or something to a previous condition or place means to cause them to be in that condition or place once again. 使复原
restoration 恢复
To restore a situation or practice means to cause it to exist again. 恢复

If a rate or number creeps up to a higher level, it gradually reaches that level. 攀升
If something creeps in or creeps back, it begins to occur or becomes part of something without people realizing or without them wanting it. 悄悄出现
If something creeps somewhere, it moves very slowly. 渐渐蔓延
When people or animals creep somewhere, they move quietly and slowly. 悄悄地缓慢行进
