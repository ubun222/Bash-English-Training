modify |英 ['mɒdɪfaɪ]  美 ['mɑdɪfaɪ]| vt. 修改，修饰；更改/vi. 修改/
Relatively minor modifications were required.需要相对较小的修改。
The club members did agree to modify their recruitment policy.俱乐部成员确已同意修改他们的入会政策。


If you say that you are doing your utmost to do something, you are emphasizing that you are trying as hard as you can to do it. 最大努力
You can use utmost to emphasize the importance or seriousness of something or to emphasize the way that it is done. 最大的; 极度的
utmost |英 ['ʌtməʊst]  美 ['ʌt'most]| n. 极限；最大可能/adj. 极度的；最远的/
He would have done his utmost to help her.他本来会尽最大努力帮助她。
Security matters are treated with the utmost seriousness.安全问题受到极为严肃的对待。
It is a matter of the utmost urgency to find out what has happened to these people.当务之急是弄清楚这些人出了什么事。
